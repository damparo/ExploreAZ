Twin Arrows

Heading east from Flagstaff on I-40, you'll come across two giant arrows that will definitely make you look twice! The arrows are a part of the old Twin Arrows Trading post that sat alonside Route 66. Although the trading post is no longer in service, the Twin Arrows are worth stopping for that insta road trip pic!

How to get there: From Phx, take the I-17 N to Flagstaff. Exit on I-40 E to Twin Arrows Rd, the arrows are on the right.
